Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about my services? You’re in the right place. If you’re curious about pricing, turnaround times, file requirements, or anything else related to your project, this page is designed to give you clarity. If you don’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to reach out via Instagram Direct Message — I’m always happy to help.

1. Why are your prices so low compared to others? ⌄

I strongly believe that high-quality music production should be accessible to everyone, regardless of budget. By keeping my pricing fair and competitive, I aim to help independent artists and smaller projects achieve professional-grade sound without breaking the bank. Rest assured, the quality of work is never compromised.

2. What is the turnaround time for mixing and mastering? ⌄

Turnaround times depend on the complexity of the project. For single songs, it typically takes 3–5 business days. Larger projects such as EPs or albums may require additional time. If you have a specific deadline, please indicate it in the submission form and I will do my best to accommodate it.

3. What DAWs do you use? ⌄

I primarily use Pro Tools (certified) and FL Studio to deliver professional-quality mixes and masters. I am also highly experienced in Ableton, Logic Pro, Studio One, and Reason. This versatility allows me to work efficiently across different workflows and project formats.

4. What file formats should I send? ⌄

Please send all audio files in WAV format, preferably at 48kHz and 24-bit for optimal quality. Be sure to properly label and organize your files before submission. Refer to the upload guidelines in the submission form for naming conventions and file organization tips.

5. Can I request revisions? ⌄

Absolutely. Each project includes two complimentary revisions to ensure you are fully satisfied with the final result. Additional revisions can be accommodated for a small fee. Clear and detailed feedback will help achieve the best possible outcome.

6. What genres of music do you specialize in? ⌄

I work across a wide range of genres including Hip-Hop, Pop, EDM (House, Drum & Bass, Dubstep, and more), Rock, Jazz, and beyond. No matter the style, I am committed to delivering a mix and master that complements your unique sound and artistic vision.

7. Do you offer discounts for EPs or albums? ⌄

Yes. I offer customized pricing for larger projects such as EPs and full-length albums. Fill out the order form for a quote tailored specifically to your project’s scope and needs.

8. What happens if I’m not satisfied with the final mix or master? ⌄

My goal is to ensure every client is fully satisfied. If adjustments are needed, we will address them within the scope of the original request. Revisions are included as outlined above to make sure the final product aligns with your expectations.

9. Do you provide recording services? ⌄

Yes. If you are located in the greater Los Angeles area, I offer professional recording sessions designed to capture your best performance. Clients are responsible for booking studio time in advance. I can also accommodate select house-call sessions with portable equipment for streamlined setups.

10. How do I get started? ⌄

Getting started is simple. Fill out the order form available on this website, upload your files, and you will be contacted to confirm project details before work begins. If you have any questions beforehand, feel free to reach out via Instagram Direct Message.
